Description

The project is to simulate a Pick-n-Place task based on a mobile base(with a 5R robotic arm being installed on it). The trajectory for the end-effector of the youBot mobile manipulator was programmed by MATLAB code.

The software was written to perform odometry as the chassis moves and feedback control to drive the youBot to pick up a block at a specified location, carry it to a desired location, and put it down.
