Biography

Dawei Liu

Dawei Liu is a second-year master's student at Northwestern University of Mechanical engineering. He's doing his ME 499 Project with his advisor Nick, aiming to write a functional program to control the motion of a robotic arm. He was born in Jining, Shandong Province, China in a hot summer. He had his eduction in hometown until senior high school. Then he went to the University of Nottingham Ningbo China and received bachelor's degree. During his college life, he finished several projects with the help of his professor, such as Pick-n-Place machine manufacturing, friction clutch designing, Inerter-based Vibration Isolation Structure designing. He was also doing a research on Human-Machine Interaction of Autonomous Vehicle. Moreover, he received Head's and Dean's scholarship each year during his college life.

He is really interested in control and robotics. During his gradution life, he spent most of time studying and doing research in this fleid. In hie spare time, he loves playing basketball and reading science fiction novel.
